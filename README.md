***Display	GPIO***

-GND	GND

-VCC/VDD	VCC / 3.3V

-SCL/CLK/SCK	GPIO 4 (D2)

-SDA	GPIO 5 (D1)

***Button	GPIO***

-UP	GPIO 14 (D5)

-Down	GPIO 12 (D6)

-A	GPIO 13 (D7)

***NEOPIXEL LED	GPIO***

-GND	GND

-VCC	VCC/3.3V

-DIN	GPIO 9 (SD2)
